    Video needs sign-in / age-restricted?
    “Sign in to confirm your age” is a hard gate — YouTube now requires a logged-in session and there’s no reliable no-login bypass (YoinkT auto-retries the old client tricks; they’re mostly patched). Export a cookies.txt from a logged-in browser (a “Get cookies.txt” extension) — a throwaway Google account works fine, it just needs to be signed in — save it in the project folder, then:
    
COOKIES_FILE=./cookies.txt ./run-local.sh

    Per-site cookies also work: COOKIES_FILE_YOUTUBE, _INSTAGRAM, _FACEBOOK, etc. This also unlocks members-only + premium-bitrate formats.
